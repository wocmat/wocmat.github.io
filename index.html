About WOCMAT

International Workshop on Computer Music and Audio Technology 2017 (WOCMAT 2017) welcomes music and paper submissions which correspond to the computer music or audio technology related subjects. WOCMAT, established in 2005 at National Taiwan University, has been hosted annually by music, computer science or engineering departments/schools at various universities in Taiwan, and has gathered international researchers, scholars and renowned guests from both the music and technology domains. WOCMAT 2017 will be organized by Master's Program in Music Engineering and Applied Music at National Tsing Hua University in November 2017.

以科技發展和人文藝術結合的「國際電腦音樂與音訊技術研討會」（International Workshop on Computer Music and Audio Technology，簡稱 WOCMAT）在台灣已經邁入第十三個年序。這個在台灣唯一大型結合電腦科技音訊技術與聲音音樂藝術的學術暨展演年會除了為台灣當前最新科技藝術發展的指標，也提供了台灣與國際科技藝術交流和觀摩的平台。 WOCMAT 是一個以電腦音樂、音樂科技與音訊技術多元結合與發表為主軸的國際型會議。這是一個發起於臺灣，以創造、演奏新的電腦音樂作品、發表創新音樂科技、以及新開發音訊技術為主軸之國際會議。
